I'M Alexander

I am a professional UX/UI designer specializing in user experience and interface design. By combining creativity and technical skills, I create designs that deliver exceptional user experiences. My main goal is to meet users’ needs through modern and functional interfaces, simplifying their interaction with digital products. When I started my design career, I understood the importance of deeply analyzing user requirements and turning them into technical solutions. I always apply a user-centered approach during the design process. For this, I utilize methods like user research, prototyping, and iteration. I also carefully balance colors, typography, and visual harmony to create aesthetically pleasing interfaces. In recent years, I have worked on projects across various fields. My portfolio includes e-commerce platforms, mobile applications, and websites. I don’t just create visually appealing designs but also analyze user behavior to ensure functionality and ease of use. I value teamwork and closely collaborate with developers and project managers. My strong communication skills and ability to express design principles in technical terms ensure the successful completion of projects. My interest in modern technologies constantly motivates me to seek innovative solutions. I am proficient in tools such as Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD, and I also use After Effects for animations and micro-interactions. In my free time, I gather inspiration by attending art exhibitions and enhancing my knowledge through design blogs. My ultimate goal is to become a leader in my field and deliver even better experiences to users.
